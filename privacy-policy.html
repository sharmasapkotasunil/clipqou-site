Privacy Policy for Clipqou

Last updated: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Clipqou ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Clipqou does not require account registration or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

When you use the app, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including your device type, operating system, IP address, and app usage data. This is collected through the third-party services described below.

Videos

Clipqou allows you to play videos by pasting a link or code. Videos played this way are streamed directly from our servers and are not uploaded by users at this time. We do not store, process, or have access to any video content you play from your local device. Local video playback happens entirely on your device.

Local Files

When you choose to open a local video file from your device gallery, the file is accessed only to play it within the app. We do not upload, copy, or transmit local files to any server. The file remains entirely on your device.

Firebase

We use Google Firebase for remote app configuration and diagnostics. Firebase may collect anonymous device and usage data. You can review Google's privacy practices at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Advertising — Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. For more information visit https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store any video content or personal data on our servers, there is no user-submitted content to retain or delete. Anonymous technical usage data collected by Firebase and AdMob is governed by their respective retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Clipqou is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since the app does not allow content uploads or submissions, no user-generated content is stored on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
